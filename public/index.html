Platform Features

Everything You Need, All in One Place

A comprehensive digital platform built to serve lakhs of commuters across Gujarat's road transport network.

📝

Online Application

Apply for new bus passes from home. Fill the form, upload documents, and submit — all digitally within minutes.

🔄

Easy Renewal

Renew your existing pass with just a few clicks. Your previous records are auto-populated for convenience.

📥

Instant Download

Download your approved digital pass as a PDF instantly. Print it or show on your phone for verification.

📍

Live Tracking

Track your application status in real-time — from submission to verification, approval, and issuance.

💳

Secure Payment

Integrated payment gateway supporting UPI, net banking, and cards for hassle-free pass fee payment.

🛡️

Admin Dashboard

GSRTC staff can verify, approve, or reject applications efficiently with a powerful admin panel.

Step-by-Step Process

Get Your E-Pass in 4 Simple Steps
